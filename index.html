Pijr is Ukrainian rock band from Kiyv. Formed in 2021, the group's line-up includes vocalist MeatEat & Nazik, guitarist Strakh,drummer Rofl,bass Asn.Due to Pijr`s incorporation the band is generally described as a style-transcending act and part of progressive rock, psychedelic rock, and art rock. The relationship between the band and today's music industry is ambivalent, at times marked by censorship, and the band's insistence on privacy.
